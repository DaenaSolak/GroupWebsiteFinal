The DAUNT. Vision

Feminism is about fighting for the rights of each women to be fully respected and enforced as part of a larger struggle for social justice, sustainable development and human rights for all. This is not about ‘adding women’ into existing unequal structures. Feminism is about social transformation. We need to address intersecting unequal power relations and oppose unjust ones, including economic, environmental, social, sexual, racial, generational and ethnic discrimination.

We want to create a world with our movement where the rights and wellbeing of women are put before profit. Our mission is to support feminist, women’s rights and gender justice movements to thrive, to be a driving force in challenging systems of oppression, and to co-create feminist realities. DAUNT. values: INTEGRITY, DIVERSITY, INCLUSIVITY and UNITY. We are not here to exclude anyone.

DAUNT. is UNIQUE

You will not find a brand like DAUNT. out there. Our whole motto is: ''We don't accept you despite of your race and class. We accept you BECAUSE of your race and class. We want you to be unapologetically yourself and to not feel like your heritage is something to hide from the world.
